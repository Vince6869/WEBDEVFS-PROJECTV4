Sova

Sova, a master of reconnaissance, uses his bow and specialized abilities to reveal enemies and gather critical information for his team. His arrows allow him to see through walls and disorient opponents, making him an invaluable asset in any battle.

Special Abilities

C - Recon Bolt

EQUIP a sonar bolt that sticks to surfaces and pulses, revealing enemy positions in the vicinity. Activate the bolt to send out a pulse that locates enemies behind walls or in hidden areas.
