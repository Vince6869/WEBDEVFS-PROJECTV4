Phoenix

Phoenix is a self-sufficient duelist who excels at healing and damage-dealing, using fire-based abilities to control the battlefield. His abilities allow him to heal himself, create blinding flashes, and resurrect after death—turning him into a formidable force in any combat situation.

Special Abilities

C - Blaze

EQUIP a wall of fire that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through it. The wall can be curved by holding the fire button to create tactical obstacles.
