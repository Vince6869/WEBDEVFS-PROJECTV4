Sage

A peaceful soul turned protector, Sage is a powerful healer with the ability to revive fallen teammates and control key points of the battlefield with her defensive abilities. She's a crucial support character for any team, ensuring survival and providing control over critical areas.

Special Abilities

C - Healing Orb

EQUIP a Healing Orb that heals allies over time when used. FIRE to heal yourself or teammates, providing recovery from damage to help hold positions or prepare for the next battle.
