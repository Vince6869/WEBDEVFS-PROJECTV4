Cypher

Cypher, an expert in surveillance and intel gathering, uses his gadgets to control the flow of the battlefield. His tools provide valuable information, allowing him to protect his team and gather intel on enemy movements.

Special Abilities

Trapwire

EQUIP a trap that is placed on the ground. Once triggered, the trap binds and slows the enemy while emitting an audible sound to alert teammates.
