Viper

Viper is a controlling agent who uses toxins and traps to manipulate the battlefield. With her skillset, she can block vision, slow down enemies, and deal damage with her toxic abilities. She excels in area denial and punishing enemies who try to cross her toxic zones.

Special Abilities

Poison Cloud

EQUIP a canister that can be thrown to deploy a cloud of toxic gas. The cloud can be activated remotely to block vision and deal damage over time to enemies caught within it.
